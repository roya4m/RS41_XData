How to get WMO station block and station index?

Use Vaisala.Soundings.Framework.ISounding.GetParameterValue() to aquire the following parameters:

1 strBlockIndex = int(self.SoundingInterface.GetParameterValue('Sounding.Station.WMO.WmoBlockNumber'))
2 strStationIndex = int(self.SoundingInterface.GetParameterValue('Sounding.Station.WMO.WmoStationNumber'))
